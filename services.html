Digital Employees

Intelligent AI agents that live inside secure containers, connected to your systems. They can read emails, draft responses, schedule meetings, update records, and make decisions within parameters you define.

Best for: inbox management, front desk operations, client communication, complex decision workflows.

  • Natural language understanding
  • Context-aware responses
  • Integration with CRM, calendar, email
  • Human escalation when needed

Automation Machines

Purpose-built containers running specialized software, enhanced with LLM connectivity for lightweight reasoning. These are your workhorses — reliable, scalable, and designed for repetitive operational tasks.

Best for: data processing, report generation, inventory checks, routine operational tasks.

  • Scheduled and event-triggered execution
  • Multi-system data synchronization
  • Error handling and retry logic
  • Full audit logging

Simple Integrations

Not every problem needs an AI brain. Sometimes you just need two systems to talk to each other. We build clean, maintainable webhook and API integrations via Zapier, Pabbly Connect, or direct connections.

Best for: form-to-CRM syncing, Slack notifications, invoice automation, simple alerts.

  • Zapier & Pabbly Connect flows
  • Custom webhook endpoints
  • API bridging between legacy tools
  • Low maintenance, high reliability
Our Process

How We Work

We believe in transparency, collaboration, and doing the hard work of understanding your business before we write a single line of automation.

📋 Requirements, Clarified

We send you a structured requirements document — not a blank page. For every item, we include LLM-generated explanations of what it is, why we need it, and where to find it in your systems. Most clients complete this in under an hour.

🤖 Self-Service Guidance

Stuck on a requirement? Our bot guides you through finding the information. It knows your stack, speaks your language, and won't judge you for asking "where do I find the API key?"

🙋 Human Help, When You Need It

If the bot can't get you unstuck, we step in. Human-to-human guidance is always available — because some questions deserve a conversation, not a chatbot.

🚀 Deploy, Monitor, Optimize

We deploy into your sovereign infrastructure, train your team, and stay on call. As your business evolves, so do your automations. We're not a vendor — we're a partner.
