Section 3: The Pain & Priority Matrix

Not all automations are equal. Use this matrix to identify which tasks hurt the most and which would be easiest to fix.

Scoring Guide

Time Consumed (1–5): 1 = under 30 min/week, 5 = 5+ hours/week

Energy Drain (1–5): 1 = neutral, 5 = soul-crushing

Error Risk (1–5): 1 = mistakes don't matter, 5 = mistakes are costly

Automation Ease (1–5): 1 = very complex, 5 = simple and well-defined

Interpretation: Tasks with high Time + Drain + Risk scores AND high Ease scores are your "quick wins." Tasks with high scores but low Ease may need custom automation. Bring this to our call and we'll help you prioritize.
