About

We Don't Replace People.
We 10X Their Productivity.

Clock Lobster was founded on a simple belief: your team's time is too valuable to spend on repetition. Our mission is to give that time back — so your people can do the work that only they can do.

Our Story

Why We Exist

"I started Clock Lobster because I saw brilliant people spending their days on tasks a machine could handle — and I knew they had so much more to offer."

I kept seeing the same pattern everywhere: businesses hiring talented people, then handing them hours of repetitive admin work. The front desk person who had a knack for operations but spent all day on emails. The analyst buried in data entry instead of finding insights. The manager who wanted to mentor their team but was stuck sorting spreadsheets every Monday morning.

These people weren't underperforming — they were underwater. And the fix wasn't more hours or more headcount. It was giving them their time back.

That's why I built Clock Lobster: to be the partner that handles the repetitive work so your people can do their best work. We build digital employees with the same care you'd put into hiring a real one — with security built in, legal oversight at every step, and a commitment to protecting the people our technology serves.

— Victor, Founder

Philosophy

What We Believe

🌱 More Human, Not Less

When a digital employee takes over the inbox, your team doesn't become less important — they become more available. Available to build relationships. To solve creative problems. To do the work that made them excited to join your company in the first place.

🛡️ You Own Everything

Your data. Your systems. Your rules. Every automation we build belongs to you — fully documented, fully auditable, fully in your control. No black boxes, no lock-in, no surprises.

🤝 A Partner, Not a Platform

When you work with us, you work with the actual people building your solution. No account managers rotating every quarter. No ticket queues. Just a small team that learns your business and stays invested in your success.

🎓 People Deserve Interesting Work

The best teams aren't built on repetition — they're built on curiosity. We handle the boring stuff so your people can get back to the work that challenges them, grows their skills, and makes them excited to show up on Monday.

Mission

Giving Teams Their Time Back

Clock Lobster exists to give your team back the hours they lose to repetitive work every week. We build reliable AI team members and smart automations that run around the clock inside secure, private infrastructure. The result? Your people spend less time on admin and more time on the work that drives your business forward — innovation, relationships, and the ideas that only humans can have.
